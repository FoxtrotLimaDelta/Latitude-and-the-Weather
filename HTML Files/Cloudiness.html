Latitude vs Cloudinesss


The latitude vs. cloudiness plot shows a heavy concentration of cities at zero cloudiness at the equator (0 Deg. Latitude) and scattered cloudiness elsewhere. This would correlate to the “Latitude vs. Max Temp” and the “Latitude vs. Humidity” plots; which indicated few cities with humidity at the equator, and which matches what one would think of when higher temperatures evaporate the moisture in an area thus leaving none for humidity or cloud formation.
